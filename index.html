Greetings

I'm a PhD student at the University of Groningen. I am a member of the Computational Linguistics Group, supervised by Gertjan van Noord, Arianna Bisazza and Tessa Verhoef.

My main research focuses on simulating evolution of linguistic structure using neural agents. Specifically, I want to develop methods to simulate cultural evolution of linguistic structure through neural network agent-based computational modeling, and to explore the natural of learners' bias (whether it's statistically learned or internal cognitive constraints).

I was born in Shanxi, China. I obtained my master degree in Language Intelligence and Technology at Beijing Language and Culture University in 2022 and my bachelor degree in English at Beijing Foreign Studies University in 2019.

Current Research Interest
  • Word order preferences
  • Reinforcement learning
  • Neural network-based simulation of language evolution
